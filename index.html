How do I remove line breaks from PDF text copy-paste?

When you copy text from a PDF, it often retains messy line breaks, making the formatting look broken. To fix messy PDF copy-pasting, simply paste your text into our tool. It automatically acts as a plain text converter that cleans up the layout, eliminates unwanted breaks, and helps you format text from PDF instantly.

Can I strip formatting and convert text to plain text online?

Yes! If you need to strip formatting from text online, our tool removes rich text styles, fonts, and hidden web code. Paste your content into the box to get an unformatted text output, making it easy to copy clean text into Word, Google Docs, or your code editor without style conflicts.

What makes this the fastest online word counter?

Our toolkit is built as a minimalist, lightweight Single Page Application. Unlike heavy websites, we don't load slow third-party scripts. This ensures a fast online word counter that counts characters, words, and paragraphs instantly without clutter or lag.

Is my data safe? Looking for secure text tools online?

Absolutely. We believe in privacy-friendly word counters and text tools. Everything you paste stays directly in your browser and is never sent to a server. Your text is processed entirely client-side, making this one of the most secure text tools online for sensitive documents, essays, and code.
